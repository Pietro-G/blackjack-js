Rationale:

Blackjack has been an iconic game for me growing up, regardless of where I may be. I have decided to go by the rules that I have always used, therefore the computer can be thought of as the dealer. If the player manages to get 21, he gets a notification. The computer's nash equilibrium is to minimize the loses, even if the current version only shows two players by default, I have made this project with scalability in mind. I decided to challenge myself, and utilize Java script for the first time in my career to learn something new while pursuing this challenge. Hope whoever sees this enjoys a couple rounds of good BlackJack. - Pietro
