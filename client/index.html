Rules:

1. This implementation of BlackJack emulates a single player against a computer.

4. This version of BlackJack doesn't consider 12 to equate to a score of 20 1/2.

5. The computer's AI has been programmed to emulate a casino dealer where it is simply trying to beat the player.

6. In this version of the game - both cards are visible to everyone (on the player's and dealer's end
